每次会把下列各语句合并，并用*分隔，一起发送

(x messageSeparator)(1 messageHeader)(1 delivery)[body](2 messageLength)(x messageSeparator)
messageType和messageLength都必定不包含Separator


Body部分消息结构

E:
>>客户端接收                事件
(byte eventId)[short clientId]                                 //eventId    ==1 OnClientConnect  ==2 OnClientDisconnect
                                                                            ==3 OnServerConnect  ==4 OnServerDisconnect
                                                      //[E]3在C事件触发，[E]4在ShutDowm触发(超时/D事件/主动调用)

C:
>>客户端接收                分配Id
(int Clientid)

A:
>>服务器接收
(int clientId)         设置房主权限

>>客户端接收
(bool authorityActive)


F:
>>服务器接收                函数调用
(short NOMObjectId)(byte func)[string param](short target)

>>客户端接收                函数调用
(short NOMObjectId)(byte func)[string param]


f:
>>服务器接收                创建物体
(short sendto)(bool false)(short collectionId)(string param)(short behaviourCount)(short target)

>>客户端接收                创建物体
(bool false)(short collectionId)(string param)(short idStart)
(bool true)(short collectionId)(string param)(short idStart)
