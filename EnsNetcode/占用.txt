(x messageSeparator)(1 messageHeader)(2 messageFrom)(2 messageTarget)(1 deliveyType)[body](2 messageLength)(x messageSeparator)
messageType和messageLength都必定不包含Separator
消息长度只包含(1 messageType)(2 messageTarget)[body]
